Combat Aircraft

Indigenous

HAL Tejas

Light Combat Aircraft (LCA)

  • Role: Multirole fighter
  • Commissioned: 2016
  • Armament: Air-to-air & air-to-ground missiles
  • Engine: GE F404 turbofan
  • Speed: Mach 1.6

India's first indigenously developed 4th generation fighter aircraft designed for air defense and ground attack missions.

Tejas & AMCA programs

The Indian Air Force's budget for 2025-26 focuses on critical modernization programs:

  • Procurement of 114 Multi-Role Fighter Aircraft (MRFA)
  • Additional Tejas Mk-1A squadrons
  • Upgrades to existing Sukhoi-30MKI fleet
  • New airborne early warning systems
  • Advanced missile systems
